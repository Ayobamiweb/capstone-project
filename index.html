AWARENESS AND ABUSE OF

ILLICIT

DRUGS

An illicit drug is one that is illegal to have(e.g methamphetamine,heroin),and the non-medical use of drugs that are legally available such as pain killers and sleeping pills.

using illicit drugs can lead to health problems such as

  • risk of overdose
  • chronic disease( heart and liver problems)
  • blood-borne viruses(infections e.g hepatitis and hiv)
  • lower levels of social and emotional wellbeing(mental health problems)
  • also increases suicide

The whole COMMUNITY can be affected by the negative impact associated with illicit drugs for example childrens,youth and families.it can also lead to violence,assault and crimes.

NIGERIA has a harm approach to addressing harms from illicit drugs which includes

  • Demand reduction;preventing uptake of first use and supporting people to recover
  • Supply reduction;controlling and reducing illicit drugs availability
  • Harm reduction;reducing risky behavior and providing safety settings
