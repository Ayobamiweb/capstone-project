COCAINE

cocaine is a stimulant typically found as a white crystalline powder.it is dissolved and injected,which is extremely dangerous due to the
heightened risk of heart attack and other injection related risks.

Effect

Cocaine.like amphetamine and crack,induces physical and mental arousal.
when snorted its effect are almost immediate,peaking and fading within 15 to 30 minutes.

Risk

At higher doses,users may feel anxious and panicky.the after effect of cocaine use can include fatigue and depression.
